Our Commitment to Quality

Established in 2010, Water Service has been a trusted provider of clean, safe water to homes and businesses across the region.

We source our water from natural springs and subject it to rigorous quality testing before delivery. Our modern filtration facility ensures that every drop meets strict health and safety standards.

With a fleet of 20 delivery vehicles and over 50 dedicated employees, we serve more than 1,000 clients daily, from small cafés to large hotels and residential complexes.

Our mission is simple: to provide the purest water with the best service at fair prices.
